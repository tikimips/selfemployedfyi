# SelfEmployedFYI

**Free mortgage qualification calculator for freelancers and self-employed people.**

🔗 [selfemployedfyi.vercel.app](https://selfemployedfyi.vercel.app)

---

## The Problem

Self-employed people face a painful paradox when applying for a mortgage: the same tax deductions that reduce their tax bill also reduce the income figure conventional lenders use to qualify them.

A freelancer making **$200K gross** with **$120K in deductions** appears to earn **$80K** to a conventional mortgage lender.

Most self-employed borrowers don't discover this until they're already in the mortgage process — confused and frustrated.

## What SelfEmployedFYI Does

SelfEmployedFYI calculates your exact mortgage-qualifying income and shows you your options.

**Inputs:**
- Gross annual revenue
- Business deductions/expenses
- Filing status
- Target home price & down payment
- Credit score range

**Outputs:**
- Schedule C qualifying income (what lenders actually see)
- Maximum conventional mortgage amount
- Deduction gap analysis + tax cost of closing it
- Conventional vs. Non-QM loan recommendation
- Downloadable PDF report

## Tech Stack

- **Framework:** Next.js 14 (App Router)
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
- **Database:** Supabase (lead capture)
- **PDF Generation:** jsPDF
- **Deployment:** Vercel

## Who It's For

- Freelancers and independent contractors
- Small business owners filing Schedule C
- Consultants, designers, developers, writers
- Gig workers (Uber, Airbnb, DoorDash, etc.)
- Real estate investors with complex income
- Anyone turned down for a mortgage due to "insufficient income"
